Empowering local vendors by equipping HealthCare providers with latest innovations in AI.

During this situation of global pandemic COVID19, Healthcare service is to be catered at home keeping safety concerns in mind and meeting the needs of growing demand on HealthCare infrastructure. This vision can be fulfilled by automating the HealthCare pipelines using AI for reliability, deploying the solutions on the cloud for scalability, and leveraging our Local HealthCare service providers from all ranges(Hospitals, Clinic, Self Private Practitioners, Pharmacies, Labs and others. AI as a scalable service on Cloud is the only possible solution that can make the health Industry reach directly at doorstep.

Some services:
  • We Offer various solutions deployed on cloud and customize it around your brand.
  • We Offer diagnostic services, real time EEG, ECG analytics (or Other biophysical signals).
  • We also build customized online OCD and consultation platform unique to your Brand.
  • We can also minimize contact in your logistic chain by application of IoT driven products.
  • Association with HSaaS, securing and upgrading your services, automating your pipeline, equipping your staff with AI tools will also add a layer of reliability and authenticity to your brand.

Our Offerings after beta launch

1.
EEG, ECG analytics
This is our main USP, our team is expert and highly capable in Brain Computer Interface which has led us to gain expertise in signal processing, we offer real time monitoring, tracking, warning/alerting and reporting of various signal based processes. We are also developing ranges of IoT products that can enable real time monitoring of health at the comfort of your homes. Not only Neural or cardiological signals, we can offer real time analytics on other biophysical signals too such as respiration, temperature and blood pressure.
2.
X-ray Diagnostic engine
A really deep and insightful X-ray Diagnostic engine which not only classifies an X-ray based on presence of various anomalies such as Cavity, Fibrosis, Nodules, blunting of CP angles, enlarged arteries, apical opacity, Hilar prominence and many other irregularities but also localise the area of problem and offers a pan out and zoomed in version of such areas. This is the most intelligent X-ray Diagnostic tool around, Any HSaaS vendor is equipped with such capabilities, findings may miss the human eye but it cannot miss our AI.
3.
CT-Scan tool
Our CT-Scan tool offers really deep insight on the data hidden in the CT-Scan and offers a detailed analysis of all the features to be taken care of during decision. Our Head CT scan tool offers huge resolution in localising the areas of anomalies such as Extradural hemorrhage, Subdural hemorrhage, Intraventricular hemorrhage, Cranial fracture, ruptures, cervical bruits and a lot more.
4.
Selfie based dermatological assistant
It is our most innovative tool after signal processing capabilities and second thing in our bag that we are really proud of, Now have deep dermatological insight within seconds with just an upload of an image and we deliver a deep insight on all the dermatological conditions that you could be facing. Now dermatological lab testing can be in your pocket.
5.
MRI scan tool
Our MRI scan tool can localise areas responsible for tumors, developmental anomalies, dementia, traumatic brain injury, stroke, infection, multiple sclerosis, and the causes of headache.
6.
Chatbot
Our state of the art Chatbot, which can talk with you just like a doctor and can generate a highly professional and deep report which you can share with any doctor on our platform for easier and accurate analysis.
7.
One stop Platform
A platform where you can consult with HSaaS equipped Hospitals, Doctors, Clinics, Pharmacies and labs. HSaaS adds a layer of trust, reliability and security, equipped with AI, our platform helps in reducing human error and bias. Since we are on a scalable architecture, there is no waiting time, now don’t wait for several months for an appointment as our platform can serve multiple people at the same time. If your family doctor is not on our platform, suggest him to onboard with us so that even you can leverage the experience of AI guided medical services.
